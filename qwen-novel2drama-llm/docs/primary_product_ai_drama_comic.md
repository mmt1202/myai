# Primary product line: AI drama and AI comic factory

AI drama and AI comic creation are the most important product direction for this project.

The foundation is universal, but the first major product line after the foundation becomes stable is an AI short-drama and AI comic factory. This product line should compete with products such as MagicLight, comic-drama assistants, Jimeng/Dreamina-style image/video generation workflows, and other AI content production platforms.

## Positioning

The project should not become only a generic chatbot. The universal AI foundation must support a serious production workflow for:

- AI short drama
- AI comic / manga drama
- novel-to-script conversion
- script-to-shot conversion
- character bible and role consistency
- scene bible and visual continuity
- storyboard and shot planning
- prompt generation for image/video/audio models
- multi-model orchestration
- publishing package preparation

## Relationship with the foundation

The short-drama and comic factory is not a small side feature. It is the first primary product line built on top of the universal AI foundation.

The foundation provides:

- local model runtime
- model versioning
- context management
- code-agent infrastructure
- tool calling
- Skill / plugin / MCP expansion
- multi-model routing
- evaluation and production traceability

The product line adds:

- story reasoning
- episode planning
- character consistency
- scene continuity
- shot language
- visual prompt engineering
- asset dependency management
- model routing for image, video, voice and text
- delivery workflow for short-drama and comic production

## Own model plus external models

The platform should not rely on only one model.

The local foundation model should be responsible for:

- story understanding
- reasoning
- planning
- task decomposition
- structured output
- quality control
- production orchestration

External models can be connected for:

- high-quality image generation
- image-to-video generation
- text-to-video generation
- voice generation
- music and sound effects
- subtitle and ASR
- multimodal review

This means the project can have its own model while still using the best available external models through adapters.

## Key rule

The current phase is still foundation-first. Once the foundation reaches a usable P1/P2 state, the next major enhancement wave should focus on AI short-drama and AI comic capabilities.
